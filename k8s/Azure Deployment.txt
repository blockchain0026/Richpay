1.Login
az login

2.Update kube config.
az aks get-credentials --resource-group xxx --name xxx

3.Push Image
  3-1. login to docker from the docker you download in your pc.
  3-2. docker tag [Image Id] your_username/[Image Name]:[tag]
  3-3. docker push your_username/[Image Name]:[tag]
  3-4. Update your yaml or helm chart to use your image.

4.Deploy (Please refers kubernetes.txt.)

5.Update nginx conf to allow large header.
kubectl apply -f aks-httpaddon-cfg.yaml
